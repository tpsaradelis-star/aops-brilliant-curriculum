Overview

This page highlights Brilliant courses and resources that sit beyond the core sequence. Use them for students who are ready for more challenge or who benefit from additional visual or puzzle based work.

Core Adjacent Enrichment

Exploring Data Visually

Visual work with bar charts, scatterplots, histograms, and distribution shapes. Pairs well with AoPS Prealgebra Chapter 13 data work in Grades 6 and 7.

See alignment notes

Probability in Data

Simulation and conditional probability tasks that extend AoPS counting and probability units in upper grades.

See alignment notes

Everyday Statistics

Core statistics course from the math tab. Good source of extra practice and visual tasks when students are solid on Prealgebra Chapter 13.

Advanced or Stretch Enrichment

Clustering and Classification

Conceptual introduction to similarity and grouping. Best used with small groups in Grade 7 or 8 after core statistics work.

See alignment notes

Regression

Uses scatterplots and trend lines for prediction. Suitable for a short project in Grade 8 once scatterplots are secure.

See alignment notes

Predicting with Probability

Focus on distributions and formal probability language. Use only with advanced Grade 8 students who finish AoPS probability early.

See alignment notes

Bayesian Probability

High end conceptual work. Reserve for very strong students or short seminar style experiences connected to decision making.
